01 who i am

Ten years in — from backend startups in Cairo to decentralised identity systems in Berlin — I've shipped OIDC4VP-based identity providers, re-architected services in Dart that cut response times in half, and migrated production infrastructure from Kubernetes to serverless. I lead technical initiatives, mentor engineers, and write code that has to survive the next engineer.

My focus for the past four years has been making digital identity work at infrastructure level — the protocols, the credential flows, the auth layers that let people prove who they are without giving up control of their data. That's the problem I want to keep solving.

TypeScript and Dart are my daily drivers. I reach for Rust where correctness demands go further.
