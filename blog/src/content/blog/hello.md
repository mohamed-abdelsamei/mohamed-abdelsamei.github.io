Ten years into backend engineering, most of the last four have gone into one question: how do you let people prove who they are without handing over control of their data? That's what this blog is about.

Expect posts on Verifiable Credentials and OIDC4VP in practice, the tradeoffs behind moving production infrastructure from Kubernetes to serverless, and the kind of backend design decisions that only look obvious in hindsight.

No fixed schedule. I'll write when there's something worth saying, not to fill a calendar.

Subscribe via the [RSS feed](/blog/rss.xml) if you want the next one to find you.
